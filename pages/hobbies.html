Boating

Ever since I was very young, I simply loved being on or around the water.

I've been very fortunate that my career has taken me to South Florida with the Bahamas only 50 miles away. Our family is able to go over and enjoy the beautiful, crystal clear water at least a few times a year.

There is nothing more magical in this life than jumping into 75 ft deep water and being able to see the fish at the bottom.
